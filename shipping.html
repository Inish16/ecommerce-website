Enjoy free standard delivery on all prepaid orders above ₹999. No coupon needed — discount is applied automatically at checkout.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

COD is available across India. A ₹49 COD handling fee applies to all cash-on-delivery orders. Available for orders up to ₹5,000.

Packaging

All T-shirts are carefully folded, wrapped in tissue paper, and packed in sturdy branded boxes to prevent damage during transit.

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day. Custom and bulk orders may take 2–3 additional business days for production.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking ID via email and SMS. Track your order anytime on our Order Tracking page.

Bulk Order Shipping

For bulk orders (50+ units), we offer special discounted shipping rates and dedicated logistics support. Contact us for a custom shipping quote.
