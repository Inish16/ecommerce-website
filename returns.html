RETURNS &
EXCHANGES

Your satisfaction is our priority. Easy returns within 7 days.

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If something isn't right, our simple return and exchange process has you covered — no hassle, no stress.

7-Day Return Window

You have 7 days from the delivery date to initiate a return or exchange. Just log your Order ID and we'll guide you through the process.

Conditions for Returns

  • Item must be unused and unwashed
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Original packaging required
  • No stains, odors, or damage

Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized / personalized T-shirts
  • Bulk orders (10+ units)
  • Items on clearance / sale
  • Items damaged by the customer

Refund & Exchange

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Exchanges ship out within 3–5 business days.
