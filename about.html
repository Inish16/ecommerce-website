Brand Story

FROM ONE MACHINE TO THOUSANDS OF STORIES

It started in 2018 with a single DTG printer, a 200 sq ft room, and an obsession with print quality that most vendors ignored. We believed custom T-shirts deserved the same premium treatment as luxury fashion — meticulous craftsmanship, premium materials, and attention to every thread.

Six years later, we've printed over 500,000 T-shirts for 15,000+ happy customers across India — from solo creators to Fortune 500 companies. The machine has changed. The obsession hasn't.
